Guatemala

Guatemala is a vibrant country in Central America known for its rich Mayan heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and colorful culture. Bordered by Mexico, Belize, Honduras, and El Salvador, it boasts diverse geography that includes volcanoes, rainforests, and the beautiful Lake Atitlán. The ancient ruins of Tikal stand as a testament to the country’s deep historical roots, while modern cities like Guatemala City and Antigua blend colonial architecture with lively markets and traditions. Guatemala is also famous for its textiles, coffee, and festivals that celebrate its indigenous and Spanish influences.
